Switzerland

Government Requirements

  • All people living in Switzerland must check in at their area's Kraussburo and register their presence. There is a fee associated with this registration around 200CH
  • All people living in Switzerland longer than 4 months must have Swiss Insurance. Cheapest plans start around 250CH

    • Cultural Differences

  • Most businesses close on Sundays, including grocery stores, besides those located at the train station
