Switzerland

General Information

  • Capital: Bern
  • Population: 8.4 million (2016)
  • Currency: Swiss Francs (CHF)
  • Euro () is accepted at most places

    • Language

  • Official Languages: German, French, Italian, Romansh
  • Majority of people speaks German
  • Due to high educational standards, most people also speak English

    • Government Requirements

  • All people living in Switzerland must check in at their area's Kreisburo and register their presence. There is a fee associated with this registration around 200 CHF
  • All people living in Switzerland longer than 4 months must have Swiss Insurance. Cheapest plans start around 250 CHF

    • Cultural Differences

  • Most businesses close on Sundays, including grocery stores, besides those located at the train station
  • Public transportation is a quiet space. Being loud is often considered rude
  • Swiss people are often considered reserved towards strangers and foreigners. Especially compared to more lively cultures
